Modern Trends in Nail Art for the Year 2023

Nail Art AI Technology

Clockwork is the first robot to paint nails, and I recently gave it a try. The cutting-edge technology and equipment are indicative of a broader movement toward algorithmically streamlining the classic manicure. Tom Bachik, a celebrity manicurist with over 30 years of expertise, says that there are a few businesses working on nail-painting equipment, but that progress is slow. Bringing "that kind of technology" to the user, as Bachik puts it, "has a number of impediments in place." "Because everyone's nails are shaped differently, it's challenging to develop a general algorithm for learning how to paint them evenly and precisely. Unfortunately, we still lack the necessary technologies to make this a reality."

NIMBLE A AUTOMATIC NAIL PAINTER

Nimble is a revolutionary device that can scan your nails, paint them, and dry them all at the same time. The Nimble device scans your nails in two dimensions and three to understand their precise dimensions, as well as their shape and curvature. A base coat, two coats of color, and a top coat are meticulously applied by Nimble's intelligent robotic arm for a manicure with a rich, high-shine finish that resists chips for up to 7 days.
